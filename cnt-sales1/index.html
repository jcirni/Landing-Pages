More About FastCAP Carbon Nanotubes

The CVD process tends to produce a much cleaner material compared to bulk powder manufacturing. Often there is substantial metal content mixed in with bulk powders from the catalyst, and they require additional processing to remove the contaminant. With FastCAP’s CVD method, virtually all the metal catalyst remains adhered to the substrate when the VA-CNTs are removed for use.

The growth method is also more finely controlled than most alternatives, so the proportion of carbon that has formed nanotubes instead of other amorphous carbon structures tends to be much higher.

FastCAP verifies the quality of their VA-CNTs with Raman Spectroscopy; TEM and SEM imaging; and Thermo Gravimetric Analysis.
